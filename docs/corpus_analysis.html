Text Analysis

I decied to compare two different 19th century texts. The first text I analyzed was Bram Stoker's Dracula. I compared it to Edgar Allan Poe's The Cask of Amontillado I compared the two because not only were they both written in the 19th century, but they are also both horror stories. I also think comparing the much longer Dracula to the shorter The Cask of Amontillado could reveal some interesting insights on the differences between short and long stories.
